Workshops & Tech Talks
We try to help students every week by holding interactive workshops geared toward learning by doing. These are mainly used to facilitate development of games for the quarterly game jams, and used as a workspace for people to work on their games.
Community Service
We reach out to the surrounding Los Angeles community and expose youths to the joys of game development and design.
Game Jams
We host a quarterly competition where people make games in accordance to a certain theme. We plan to do shorter, "hackathon-esque" jams in the future.
Hackathons
We are partnered with HackPoly, Cal Poly Pomona's collegiate hackathon as well as attend other collegiate hackathons nation-wide as club.
Tech Tours
We organize monthly trips to local companies we think are awesome for our members.
