Game Objective

The Game Design & Development Club is focused on facilitating game development and teamwork while providing a fun, educational, and social experience for members. We welcome students interested in different aspects of game development whether it be creating art, writing stories, composing music, programming, business and marketing, or just playing games!

Your Team
