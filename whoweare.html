Game Objective

The Game Development and Design Club is focused on facilitating game development and teamwork while providing a fun, educational, and social experience for members. We welcome students interested in different aspects of game development whether it be creating art, writing stories, composing music, programming, business and marketing, or just playing games!

Your Team

The GDDC E-board is a multidisciplinary group of students varying from Graphic Design to Electrical Engineering. Students run every year for their desired position.
