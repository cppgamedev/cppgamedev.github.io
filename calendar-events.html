Game Pitch Project!

A chance to form a group and develop a completed game by the end of the year! The goal is to eventually publish the finished game and put it on your resume! We will provide you with guidance every step of the way. Oh, there will also be opportunities to win prizes and to show off your game to professionals in the industry! Please fill out the form below if you are interested. Enter your team name, your members, and the kind of game you are going to create: http://tiny.cc/cppgamepitch If you do not yet have a team, fill out the form on your own and we will do our best to match you up with a group at one of our general meetings. To learn more about this awesome opportunity please get involved! Visit one of our general meetings, join our facebook group, and chat with fellow club members on the project's Discord.
