About the Game Pitches

Every semester, the CPP Game Development Clubs holds the Game Pitches! It's an opportunity for all members of the club to present an idea of a game to the other members, who then group together in teams to work on the project from the entire the semester. The goal is to have a minimum viable product ready to present at the Club Banquet!
