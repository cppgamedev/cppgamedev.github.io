Weekly Newsletter
Greetings Developers, 
I hope you all are doing well! As September is coming to a close, our game development teams have formed. If you're interested in joining in the development of a game, it's not too late! Whether you are looking to be an artist, programmer, writer, or designer, you can still hop in the Discord server and group up.

For those of you who are interested in game jams, we're also planning on hosting one at the end of October (from 10/30 to 11/1). For those of you who aren't familiar with the term, a 'game jam' is an event where you get to make a game over the course of a weekend. It'll be a great chance to work with new and old friends alike! We'll be unveiling more details as we approach the jam's kickoff, so stay tuned!
 
We use Discord for everything. Join the community!
